Pura Resorts, Mukteshwar

About Pura Resorts, Mukteshwar

Getting out and about on foot is just one of the ways we recommend to explore the diversity of untouched nature at Pura resorts, Mukteshwar.

Uncover the folklore behind the origination of Mukteshwar. Position yourself in the heart of town to take pleasure in the astounding views and breakfast maple syrup before indulging in caramelized mountain berries brunches. An advantage for the food gourmands out there, our extensive collection of gourmet getaways and wonderful local wines are a must companions for the evenings.

Road trips to experience to beauty of the seven lakes; Saatal is an ideal destination to choose
