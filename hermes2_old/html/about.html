About Us

Pura is a collection of holiday stays in alternate and offbeat travel destinations. Our hand-picked selection of boutique resorts, holiday homes, lodges and villas are thoughtfully designed living spaces offering unique settings for refreshing holidays amidst liveliness of nature.

When it comes to planning your holidays, taking a short break or a weekend getaway. we at Pura are committed to offer quality and standardized holiday accommodation, programmed with local experiences and activities to suit varied holiday moods. With an all day cafe, celebrating locally rich cuisines and handpicked popular cuisines from the world for your taste palettes.

Pura derives its name from "purity of Nature" depicting Symmetry in nature, which embodies into a shape that matches itself rather than being fragmented into the classical disparities of order.

The fractals in nature - flowers, snowflakes, hand impressions among others has inspired our brand ethos, as to how Pura nurtures order and harmony found in the distinctive and unpredictable foundation of Nature.

Our collection of resorts and Stays represent the finest example of holiday stays in alternative terrains of travel destinations. Whilst every property is unique, we ensure consistent quality of facilities, service and overall experience across properties, in foundation to our specialist operating procedures ensuring worry free holidays.

We ardently deliver the best holidays in alternate travel destinations and are always willing to go that extra mile to make sure you get exactly what you want, the way you want it.
